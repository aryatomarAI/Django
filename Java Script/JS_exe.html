Welcome to JavaScript Level One Project!

For this project we will be building a generic website that will seem to ask mundane questions to normal users, but we secretly are looking for a spy! Anyone visiting our website will be asked a series of questions, only the Spy will be able to give specific answers we expect. If all questions are answered correctly, we will post a secret message in console for the Spy to read!

Here are the four conditions we will check for the spy:

  • The Spy has the same first letter of her First Name and Last Name
  • The Spy is between the Age of 20 and 30 (exclusive of 20 and 30)
  • The Spy is at least 170 centimeters tall.
  • The Spy has a pet name that ends with the letter "y".

After the all requirements meet we will display a message that is "Welcome Agent to our Spy Agency"
